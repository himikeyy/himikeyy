Tips for Searching

How do I find information that matches my needs? How do I choose from the wide variety of information made available by different sources?

Determine

your main subject and brainstorm a variety of keywords that will link to your information.

Provide

a series of terms that you can search in combinations of two, three or more.

Prioritize

utilizing credible search engines like Google Scholar for better search results and evaluated sources.

Certify

the relevance of the information while verifying the credibility and reliability of the chosen source.

Evaluate

the purpose and intended audience that is being offered by the information.

Approximate

the accuracy of your acquired informations that will conclude your content.
